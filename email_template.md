<h2 style="color: #2c3e50; margin-top: 30px;">BROKER CE Requirements (April 30, 2026)</h2>

<h3 style="color: #555; margin-top: 20px;">If License Issued BEFORE November 1, 2023:</h3>
<p><strong>12 hours total:</strong></p>
<ul style="margin: 10px 0; padding-left: 20px;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">6-hour Core course (now includes 2 hours of Fair Housing training)</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">6 hours of electives (must include 1 hour Sexual Harassment Prevention Training)</li>
</ul>
<p style="background: #fff3cd; padding: 12px; border-left: 4px solid #ffc107; margin: 15px 0;"><em>Note: If you completed a 4-hour Core course between May 1, 2024 and June 30, 2025, it still counts - but you'll need 8 hours of electives instead of 6.</em></p>

<h3 style="color: #555; margin-top: 20px;">If License Issued Between November 1, 2023 and October 31, 2025 (First-Time Renewal):</h3>
<p><strong>45 hours Post-License Education</strong> - NOT regular CE. This is three 15-hour courses covering:</p>
<ul style="margin: 10px 0; padding-left: 20px;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Applied Brokerage Principles</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Risk Management/Discipline</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Transactional Issues</li>
</ul>
<p><em>(Sexual Harassment Prevention Training is included in the 45-hour curriculum)</em></p>

<h3 style="color: #555; margin-top: 20px;">If License Issued ON OR AFTER November 1, 2025:</h3>
<ul style="margin: 10px 0; padding-left: 20px;">
<li><strong>No renewal required in 2026</strong> - your first renewal is April 30, 2028</li>
</ul>

<h2 style="color: #2c3e50; margin-top: 30px;">LEASING AGENT CE Requirements (July 31, 2026)</h2>

<h3 style="color: #555; margin-top: 20px;">If License Issued BEFORE May 1, 2024:</h3>
<ul style="margin: 10px 0; padding-left: 20px;">
<li><strong>8-hour Residential Leasing Agent Core Course</strong> (includes the required 1 hour of Sexual Harassment Prevention Training)</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color: #555; margin-top: 20px;">If License Issued ON OR AFTER May 1, 2024:</h3>
<ul style="margin: 10px 0; padding-left: 20px;">
<li><strong>No CE required for this renewal</strong> - but still must pay fee and submit renewal</li>
</ul>

<h2 style="color: #2c3e50; margin-top: 30px;">ATTORNEYS - The Exemption</h2>
<p>Attorneys admitted to practice law pursuant to Illinois Supreme Court rule are exempt from CE, but must certify on the renewal application full compliance with the CE requirements.</p>
<p><strong>What this means:</strong></p>
<ul style="margin: 10px 0; padding-left: 20px;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">No courses required (no CE, no post-license education)</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>STILL must submit renewal application</strong></li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;"><strong>STILL must pay the renewal fee</strong> ($200 for brokers, $250 for managing brokers)</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 8px;">Must certify compliance on the application form</li>
</ul>

<h2 style="color: #2c3e50; margin-top: 30px;">How to Renew After Completing CE</h2>
<ol style="margin: 10px 0; padding-left: 20px;">
<li style="margin-bottom: 10px;"><strong>Complete CE</strong> through an IDFPR-approved provider (they report your completion electronically)</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 10px;"><strong>Access the IDFPR Online Services Portal:</strong> <a href="https://online-dfpr.micropact.com/" target="_blank" style="color: #3498db;">https://online-dfpr.micropact.com/</a></li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 10px;"><strong>Log in</strong> (you'll get an email with PIN about 60-90 days before deadline, or use SSN + DOB)</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 10px;"><strong>Find your license</strong> under "My Licenses" and click renew</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 10px;"><strong>Certify compliance</strong> with CE requirements</li>
<li style="margin-bottom: 10px;"><strong>Pay the fee</strong></li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Processing time:</strong> Allow 2-4 business days for license to update</p>
